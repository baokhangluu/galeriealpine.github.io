An introduction to modelling clay using the sight-size technique, under the expert guidance of Florentine sculptor Vincenzo Romanelli who represents the fifth generation in his family to follow the tradition. Open for beginners to intermediate level.

Workshops available for both adults and children. Choose a one-off workshop or 2 to 3 days. For booking: info@galeriealpine.com
