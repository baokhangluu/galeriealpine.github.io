Lee is a photographer originally from Northern England and now living and working in Villars-sur-Ollon. His photography is a hybrid of traditional analog medium format plus bespoke digital printing and mounting.

Lee's photography is typically based around long term projects including locally based ones, such as with Ski Club Villars and a study of surrounding ski resorts in the summer, to longer more personal projects of things he is interested in.

Lee est un photographe originaire du nord de l'Angleterre et vit maintenant et travaille à Villars-sur-Ollon. Sa photographie est un hybride de format moyen analogique traditionnel, plus une impression et un montage numérique sur mesure.
