Robin Scott is a Swiss graphic designer and illustrator originating from Canterbury, England, but he has lived nearly 20 years in Switzerland. He is inspired by the beauty of the Villars-Gryon region and the characters he meets. His amaz- ing graphics and digital creations are inspired by vintage tourist posters which he has married with his own distinctive contemporary style.

Robin Scott est graphiste et illustrateur suisse ori- ginaire de Canterbury en Angleterre, mais vit depuis près de 20 ans en Suisse. Il a une passion de la beauté de la région de Villars-Gryon. Ses créations sont étonnantes, alliant un style contemporain, mais aussi rétro, inspiré des anciennes affiches vantant le tourisme alpin.
